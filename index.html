Healthy Eating Habits

Eat a Variety of Nutrient-Rich Foods

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats in your diet.

Limit Processed Foods and Sugary Drinks

Avoid processed foods high in added sugars, salt, and unhealthy fats. Opt for water or unsweetened beverages instead of sugary drinks.

Portion Control

Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid overeating. Use smaller plates and bowls to help control portion sizes.

Cook at Home

Prepare meals at home using fresh ingredients whenever possible. This gives you more control over what you eat and allows you to make healthier choices.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated. Limit consumption of sugary drinks and alcohol.

Listen to Your Body

Eat when you're hungry and stop when you're full. Pay attention to hunger and fullness cues.

Be Mindful of Snacking

Choose healthy snacks like fruits, vegetables, nuts, and yogurt. Avoid mindless snacking in front of the TV or computer.
